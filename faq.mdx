<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Genie Analyst?">
    Genie Analyst is a secure, enterprise-grade AI analytics platform that allows organizations to interact with their data using natural language. It connects to your databases, vaults, and cloud systems to generate instant, validated business insights — all within a fully private and compliant environment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Genie Analyst different from other AI tools?">
    Unlike public AI tools that use shared or external models, Genie Analyst operates on a Private Multi-LLM architecture — ensuring complete data privacy and security. It never sends your data to third-party servers, never trains on your information, and follows enterprise compliance standards such as SOC 2 Type II, TX-RAMP, and GDPR (in progress).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Genie Analyst integrate with our existing systems?">
    Yes. Genie Analyst seamlessly integrates with major platforms like SAP ERP, MongoDB, Azure SQL, Google Sheets, AWS, and other enterprise databases through secure connectors.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can benefit from Genie Analyst?">
    Genie Analyst is designed for executives, analysts, finance teams, operations managers, sales, marketing, and compliance officers — anyone who relies on data to make informed business decisions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Genie Analyst ensure data privacy?">
    All data interactions are encrypted in transit and at rest. Genie never stores or uses customer data for training purposes. Every query runs within your organization’s environment or Genie’s private cloud, ensuring full compliance and control.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Genie Analyst generate insights?">
    When a user submits a prompt, Genie connects to authorized databases, retrieves relevant data, cleans and validates it, then applies Private Multi-LLM reasoning. If needed, it performs secure web lookups — and if a definitive answer isn’t available, Genie never guesses; it will ask clarifying questions instead of providing false information.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are Genie’s main use cases?">
    Genie Analyst supports a wide range of enterprise applications, including: Automated financial reporting and forecasting, operational analytics, predictive modeling, marketing performance tracking, and compliance reporting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Genie Analyst be deployed on-premise?">
    Yes. Genie supports cloud, hybrid, and on-premise deployments. Enterprises with sensitive data can host Genie within their own infrastructure or in a hybrid setup using Azure Cloud.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to implement Genie Analyst?">
    Implementation timelines vary by organization size and system complexity, but most enterprises are fully operational within 2–4 weeks, thanks to Genie’s modular and API-driven integration approach.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if Genie cannot find the right answer?">
    Genie will never provide incorrect or fabricated answers. Instead, it will transparently ask follow-up questions or request clarification to ensure the final output is accurate and contextually relevant.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Genie Analyst require technical knowledge to use?">
    No. Genie is designed for both technical and non-technical users. Anyone can simply type a question (e.g., 'Show me Q3 sales by region') and Genie automatically processes and visualizes the answer.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Genie Analyst maintained and updated?">
    Genie’s system is continuously updated with enhanced compliance modules, new connectors, and model improvements — all delivered securely via encrypted update channels.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
